Gantavya 2022

About the events

Gantavya 2022 is being organised in which several competitions are held by our d'coders club.

Events name :

  1. Bug Spotter
  2. Track Mania
  3. Problem Mania

  • Bug Spotter

    This event will be a coding competition. 5 questions will be alloted to each team. A team should have two members. One hour time will be given to the participants particularly 12 mins for each question. The questions will be giving an output but there will be logical errors in the code.

  • Track Mania

    This event is an online event which will be played on laptops. Every participant should bring their own laptop. This is a solo participant competition. You will be provided with a track and the one who will complete it in least time will be declared as the winner. 15 minutes will be provided for each track.

  • Problem Mania

    This event will take submissions online. The given questions are needed to be solved and the code is to be submitted as responses. After the deadline, no response will be accepted.

Rules and Regulations

  2. Any form of communication between teams are not allowed during the event.
