About the event

Webbed In is a web development competition being organised by GeeksforGeeks SRMGPC Student Chapter. The competition will be comprised of two rounds :

  • Round 1

    Round 1 will start at 1200hrs on 10 September 2021 and end at 1800hrs on 11 September 2021. A theme will be declared upon the start of the competition and the participants will have 30 hours to make and submit their creation on the submission portal

  • Round 2

    Round 2 will start at 1200hrs on 12 September 2021 and end at 1800hrs on 12 September 2021. A theme will be declared upon the start of the competition and the participants will have 6 hours to make and submit their creation on the submission portal

Rules and Regulations

  1. The submission will have to be made within the given timeframe.
  2. Use of pre-made templates is not permitted and the submission using them will be disqualified.
  3. Only the following three CSS frameworks are allowed:
    • Bootstrap
    • Materialize
    • Tailwind
  4. Refrain from using copyrighted material in your creations.
  5. Making fullstack applications is allowed, however doing so will not provide you any advantage over purely frontend based applications
  6. The websites will have to be hosted online and be publicly accessible for the submission to count.

Judging Criteria

Submissions will be judged in the following criteria

  • Design
  • Content
  • Technological Aspects and Interactivity
  • Creativeness/Originality
  • Loading Time
  • Responsiveness

Submission Guidelines

  1. The website created must conform to the above listed rules & regulations of the contest to be deemed as a "Valid Submission".
  2. The submission should be made strictly under the provided deadlines. No submissions shall be accepted post the provided deadlines.
  3. The participants need to submit their entry at the submission portal provided on the event website. The website link should have a public access.
  4. Kindly ensure that all the personal details & information provided with the submission form are true to your knowledge.
  5. The participants would also need to attach a short video or a power point presentation(PPT) or a description explaining how their website justifies & fits the theme of the contest.
  6. Integrity & Honesty is expected from all participants.
  7. Participation in the contest implies that participants accept these rules. Non-compliance with the rules will result in the automatic cancellation of their participation in the contest and of the candidacy for the prize.The organizers of the contest reserve all the rights in this regard.

About Us

The GeeksforGeeks SRMGPC Student Chapter is a community organization which promotes technical education in the campus. With the help of various workshops, quizzes, webinars etc it aims to guide and help students to learn and achieve more in their field. GeeksforGeeks Student Chapters are university-based community chapters for students interested in Computer Science and other core technical competencies. It provides the students an opportunity to grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and learn building effective and optimized solutions for their community as per the latest technology trends.
